Bruce Nauman, Stadium Piece

When thinking about what to propose for Western's campus, Nauman became interested in the area just south of the steps between Parks Hall and the Environmental Studies building.

Parks Hall houses the College of Business and speech pathology. The steps may have inspired Nauman to think of this area as the University's "front door"—a public entry point. He also began to reflect on the cultural symbolism of front stairs and how they could be explored in a more personal, artistic way.

Earlier in his academic life, Nauman focused on math, physics, music, and philosophy. Later, as he began creating art, he drew on this broad background. He found a unifying approach that connected these disciplines and informed his artistic practice.
